THE ALL NEW THAR

The Thar is a limited-edition variant of the iconic Thar off-road SUV.The Thar Rox is built for extreme off-roading enthusiasts, offering unparalleled capability and style. It's a tribute to the Thar's legendary off-road prowess and a statement of adventure and freedom. With only a limited number of units available, the Thar Rox is a rare and coveted possession for true off-road aficionados.

It's designed to be an ultimate off-roading machine, with features like:
- A more aggressive exterior design
- 18-inch alloy wheels with high-profile tyres
- Raised suspension for increased ground clearance
- Unique Rox badging and decals
- Exclusive interior trim and upholstery
- Enhanced infotainment system
